Cariqa, a startup founded by former HERE Technologies employees, is simplifying the fragmented EV charging experience by integrating with HERE WeGo. The combined platform lets drivers discover chargers and pay seamlessly within their navigation app, while giving charging operators direct control over pricing and customer relationships.
